# Faratech Medical Engineering Platform PRD

## Vision

Transform Faratech from a simple product showcase website into a world-class Medical Engineering Platform specializing in:

* Electric Wheelchairs
* Patient Lifts
* Mobility Scooters
* Rehabilitation Equipment
* Engineering Solutions

The website must convey engineering excellence, trust, innovation and medical professionalism.
